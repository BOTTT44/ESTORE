Where We Get Our Products

Colombo Venue

Green Farms: Located just outside the city, Green Farms is a picturesque agricultural haven known for its lush fields and fresh produce. With acres of fertile land dedicated to organic farming, Green Farms cultivates a wide variety of vegetables, from crispy lettuce to vibrant bell peppers. Their commitment to sustainable practices ensures that every vegetable is grown with care, without the use of harmful chemicals. At Green Farms, you can trust that you're getting the freshest and healthiest vegetables straight from the farm to your table.

Kandy Venue

Harvest Market: Nestled in the heart of downtown, Harvest Market is a bustling hub for locally sourced vegetables. Here, you'll find an extensive selection of seasonal produce carefully curated from nearby farms and growers. From juicy tomatoes picked at peak ripeness to crisp cucumbers bursting with flavor, Harvest Market prides itself on offering the best of the region's harvest. With friendly staff eager to assist and a vibrant atmosphere, shopping at Harvest Market is not just about buying vegetables; it's an experience that celebrates the abundance of nature.

Matale Venue

Sunshine Grocers: Serving the community for over two decades, Sunshine Grocers is a trusted name in providing quality vegetables at affordable prices. With multiple locations across the city, Sunshine Grocers sources its vegetables from a network of trusted suppliers who prioritize freshness and quality. From everyday staples like carrots and onions to exotic finds like heirloom tomatoes and kale, Sunshine Grocers offers a diverse range of vegetables to suit every taste and culinary need. With a commitment to customer satisfaction and a dedication to supporting local farmers, Sunshine Grocers is your go-to destination for fresh and flavorful vegetables.
